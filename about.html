About

A studio of one.

JRS Digital is a small, independent software studio. One developer, no investors, no growth targets — just apps built because they were worth building.

What we believe

01

Privacy by default

No accounts, no analytics, no selling attention. If an app doesn't need your data to work, it doesn't collect it.

02

Small & focused

Every app solves one real problem well, instead of collecting features to justify a subscription.

03

Built to last

No dark patterns to hit quarterly numbers, and no reason to collect more than an app actually needs to function.

Why solo

Every JRS Digital app is designed, built, and maintained by a single developer. That means slower shipping — but it also means no committee decisions diluting an app's purpose, and no pressure to grow a feature list or a data pipeline just to look busy for a board that doesn't exist.
