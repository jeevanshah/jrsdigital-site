PriceMinder

A memory for price rises.

Not a subscription tracker. PriceMinder remembers exactly what you pay for your internet and mobile plans — especially the promo price, and the day it quietly expires.

What it does

Tracks promo expiry

Log what you're paying now and when the discount ends, so a bill hike never catches you off guard.

Compares against the market

Checks your plans against a public, anonymous catalog of current NBN and mobile offers, so you know if you're overpaying.

Private by design

No accounts, no sign-in, no analytics. Your financial data is stored only on your device.

Privacy, actually

Everything you enter stays in local storage on your phone. There's no server collecting your subscriptions, bills, or prices — so there's nothing for us to see, sell, or lose in a breach. Read the full privacy policy.
