PriceMinder

A memory for price rises.

PriceMinder remembers exactly what every subscription costs you — streaming, software, memberships, NBN, mobile — especially the promo price, and the day it quietly expires.

What it does

One library, every subscription

Streaming, software, gym memberships, cloud storage, NBN, mobile — everything you pay for, with what it costs and when it renews.

Compares NBN & mobile plans

Checks your internet and mobile plans against a public, anonymous catalog of current offers, so you know if you're overpaying.

Private by design

No accounts, no sign-in, no analytics. Your financial data is stored only on your device.

See it in action

The Dashboard — a real screen from the app, currently in development.

Privacy, actually

Everything you enter stays in local storage on your phone. There's no server collecting your subscriptions, bills, or prices — so there's nothing for us to see, sell, or lose in a breach. Read the full privacy policy.
