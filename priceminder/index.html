PriceMinder

Catch every price hike before it costs you.

PriceMinder tracks what every subscription and bill actually costs you — streaming, software, memberships, NBN, mobile — and remembers the exact day a promo quietly expires, so you can act first instead of finding out on the invoice.

What it does

01

Never miss a price hike

Checks your internet and mobile plans against a public, anonymous catalog of current offers, so you know the moment you're overpaying.

02

One library, every subscription

Streaming, software, gym memberships, cloud storage, NBN, mobile — everything you pay for, what it costs, and when it renews.

03

Private by design

No accounts, no sign-in, no analytics. Your financial data is stored only on your device.

Privacy, actually

Everything you enter stays in local storage on your phone. There's no server collecting your subscriptions, bills, or prices — so there's nothing for us to see, sell, or lose in a breach. Read the full privacy policy.
